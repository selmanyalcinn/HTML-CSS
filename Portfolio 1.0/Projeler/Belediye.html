Home

On the homepage, you can follow the important events and news in Bursa, see the events that many prominent people prefer to attend, and see the places that many people prefer to go that day in the "Featured Places" tab.

Events

You can follow many events in Bursa such as state theaters, concerts, festivals and buy tickets for these events on the Events page.

Transportation

On the Transportation page, you can find directions showing how you can go to any place you want to go by using public transportation. You can also download a map of various places in Bursa by using the "Download Map" option and continue to get directions when you are offline. You can also use the "Wake Me Up" option if you wish. You can get a notification or vibrate your phone when you come to the stop you need to get off at. In this way, you can get rid of the problem of missing a stop in cases such as being distracted or falling asleep.
